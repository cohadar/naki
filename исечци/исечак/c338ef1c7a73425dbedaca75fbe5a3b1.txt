We talk in our culture as if screening is prevention. Screenings—such as colonoscopies, mammograms, prostate exams, routine dermatological evaluation and other screening methodologies—are not true prevention.
Screening is catching it after it’s caught you. True prevention is stopping cancer before it catches you!
